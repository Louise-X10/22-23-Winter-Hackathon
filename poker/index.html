Multi-player Poker Card Game

Refresh page until pop-up window asking for username appears!

Instructions:

  • Player's cards can be flipped by clicking any time during the game.
  • Each game consists of 4 rounds. In each round, players take turns making a bet. The common cards are automatically revealed once a round is finished.
    • Round 0: no cards flipped
    • Round 1: flip first three cards
    • Round 2: flip fourth card
    • Round 3: flip last card
  • In each player's turn, the players can bet tokens by selecting tokens and pressing the 'Make Bet!' button. The player can also choose to fold and quit this game. A light green border around your table will appear to signify it's your turn.
  • Throughout the game, there will be a message area (in bright colors) at the bottom left displaying the current state of the game and reminders for what to do.
  • Once all rounds are done, a pop-up window will display the winner. This message will also be shown in the message area of the webpage.
  • Then winners can collect tokens and non-winners can clear the tokens by pressing the corresponding buttons.
  • A new game will be initiated once ALL players have clicked the 'Next Game' button.

Simplified poker rules for each round:

  • For each round, there are maximum two cycles. During the first cycle through all the players, raises (i.e. bet more than previous players) are allowed. In the second cycle (if needed), only matches (i.e. in a single round, all players' total bet value matches the highest total bet value) are allowed.
  • Play order is determined by the order users entered their username. Play order will be rotated when a new game starts.

How to start playing:

  • Game starts when ANY ONE player clicks 'Start button'.
  • Anyone can click `Reset` to start a brand new game, but all players will start fresh, all previous tokens won and lost will all be rest.
  • After each complete game, players MUST click the collect/clear token button before clicking the next game button.
  • NOTE: Ties between more than 2 players will break the game.
